Rules

  • Each person starts with £100.00!
  • Total of 10 races
  • Minimum bet is £1.00
  • Winnings given to the nearest £1.00 (e.g £2.35 = £2.00 or £5.50 = £6.00)
  • Winning bets will return the original stake as well as the winnings
  • Bet on as many horses as you like
  • Have the most money at the end to win!
  • Integrity is important to the game! Do not cheat

Tips

  • Take a note of the horse number
  • Look at all the stats
  • Don't leave all your betting untill the last race, it might be too late by then
  • Take note of the race distance (miles and furlongs)
  • Odds are decimal:- e.g 6 = 6/1 or 2.5 = 5/2
  • Basically £1 will return whatever the odds say. So odds of 3.5 will return £3.50 per pound bet
  • Odd are given as the starting price
  • If a horse has no odds it means it didn't start
